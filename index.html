Frequently Asked Questions About Pets

1. What should i feed my pet?

There are different types of pet food, like dry food, wet food, and even grain-free foods. Your veterinarian can help you identify the best choice for your dog or cat. Different foods provide various health benefits, from supporting dental health to managing weight gain. Your vet can also offer suggestions based on your pet’s age, as nutrition needs change during your pet’s life.

2. What common diseases are my pets susceptible to?

Your veterinarian can help you understand what diseases your pet could be at risk for, from heart disease to dental disease to conditions that can be avoided with routine vaccinations. Once you know the risks, you can better help your pet stay safe and healthy from the vast majority of preventable diseases and conditions.

3. Should I brush my pet’s teeth?

Dental health is a huge part of maintaining your cat’s and dog’s quality of life. Ask your veterinarian how the long-term use of brushing at home or professional dental cleanings can help your pet avoid dental disease.
